The Problem

Generic marketplaces fail university students

Existing platforms are designed for everyone, which means they work well for no one in particular.

Safety concerns

Meeting strangers from the internet creates real risks. Students have been scammed, robbed, or worse through anonymous platforms.

No accountability

Anonymous sellers can disappear after a bad transaction. There is no community reputation to hold people accountable.

Inconvenient meetups

Coordinating with people across a city is time consuming. Students need a marketplace that works within their daily campus routine.

Chat Interface Screenshot

The Solution

A marketplace built for your campus

UniTrade connects verified university students within their own campus community. Every user is authenticated through their university email.

  • University email verification for all users
  • Campus based listings for convenient meetups
  • Real time chat with push notifications
  • Community accountability through profiles
Features

Core features

Built with student needs in mind, from our MVP and validated through user research.

Student Verification

Every user verifies their identity through university email authentication.

Real Time Chat

Message sellers instantly with our built in chat. No need to share personal contact info.

Smart Search

Find textbooks by course code, filter by category, or browse what is available nearby.

Push Notifications

Get notified when someone messages you or when items you want become available.

User Profiles

Build reputation through completed transactions. See seller history before buying.

Saved Items

Bookmark listings you are interested in and get alerts when prices drop.
